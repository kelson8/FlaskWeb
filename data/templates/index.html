About me and what I have been up to


I have decided not to pursue making a Minecraft or FiveM server for now, at least a public one. I may just keep the servers private between my friends and whoever wants to join it.
I have been working on learning more coding with Godot the game engine using GDScript and C#, the server stuff is fun but I just don't have the time to dedicate to doing it full time like I want and don't feel like messing with the servers.

Also I am learning way more stuff to do with Linux Administration and Windows Administration, networking, firewalls, and all kinds of stuff, I've been making projects with Raspberry Pi Picos.

I have a Raspberry Pi Pico running a IR Receiver for a remote, button #1 turns my bedroom lamp on, button #4 turns the lamp off. I am using Home Assistant for the main part of it, and ESPHome on the Raspberry Pi Pico.

Learning this stuff has helped me become the person I used to be really be, the techy side of me is really starting to show again.

I may post pictures of my projects in the future when I feel like it, eventually I will also publish the code and guides on how I am doing a lot of this stuff.
